    Family and Friends Stories

    Karli

    Brownies

    One of my favorite traditions Gabi and our friends have is our weekly movie night. We all get together, eat snacks, catch up on our weeks, and the most important part is the baked goods Gabi makes for us. I remember one night Gabi was making brownies for the group and was in a hurry to get out the door, so she took them out of the oven early. The brownies were pretty gooey but they were so good, we ate them all anyway!

    Chocolate Chip Cookies

    Every week all of our friends look forward to Tuesday night trivia at our local bar. We also look forward to Gabi’s pregame baked goods. Before we leave for trivia, we gather around Gabi’s oven and snatch up her famous chocolate chip cookies as fast as we can! I can’t think of a better way to have a pregame.
